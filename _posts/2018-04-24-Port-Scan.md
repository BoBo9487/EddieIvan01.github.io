## 编写了一个针对服务器的简易端口扫描脚本
***

## 可针对服务器进行全端口或常见端口tcp扫描

## Usage
```swift
Port Scanner
       [-h :host(ip or domain)]
       [-s :fast mode, only scan general port]
       [-w :output scan result in txt]
       E.g. port_scanner -h www.example.com -s
            port_scanner -h 127.0.0.1 -w
```
